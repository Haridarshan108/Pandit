Welcome to Pandit Ji Puja

"Pandit Chandan Ji Maharaj Aacharya, is a certified Aacharya from Banaras Hindu University (BHU) with 12 years of experience leading religious ceremonies.

He specializes in Shrimad Bhagwat Katha, Ram Katha, Akhand Ramayan, Sangeetmay Sunderkand, Griha Pravesh, Bhumi Pujan, Sathyanarayan Pujan Bhajan Sandhya Jagram, Corporate pujan and various pujan.

Pandit Chandan Ji Maharaj Aacharyai is passionate about spreading the teachings of Hinduism and contributing to the spiritual growth of the Hari Darshan community"
